                                Proxuma IT Manual

                                How the entire app works

                                Follow the workflow from first scan to final action. Each section can take you directly to the matching workspace.

                                1

                                Start a scan

                                Use the unified scan workspace to paste a URL, domain, message, or QR payload, open the camera scanner inside the same card, or choose an example target. Proxuma first performs local analysis without visiting the target.

                                2

                                Read the risk score

                                The main result combines detected signals into an overall risk level. High Risk appears red, Moderate appears orange, and Good to Go appears green. The score is a combined judgment, not a count of individual high-severity findings.

                                3

                                Use Analysis View

                                Why This Score explains the decision, Trust Timeline shows how confidence developed, Threat Story connects the signals, and Decision Guide turns the result into a clear response.

                                4

                                Open the Investigation layer

                                Overview summarizes the case. Deep Analysis expands the reasoning. Evidence organizes supporting details. Case File prepares a reusable packet that can be saved or copied.

                                5

                                Review Supporting Evidence

                                This card shows the signal mix, evidence strength, previous-scan context, and the facts behind the current result. Classified and unclassified signals should be treated separately.

                                6

                                Understand Local and Online Checks

                                Local checks inspect the target on the device. Online intelligence can later add live domain, DNS, IP, certificate, reputation, and threat-database results. A failed online source should never erase the local result.

                                7

                                Turn findings into action

                                Current Findings & Actions organizes what was detected, what matters most, and the safest next response. Use it as the operational conclusion rather than repeating instructions across multiple cards.

                                8

                                Use Optional Tools

                                Check IP, Lookup Domain, Upload File, CAPTCHA, Lookup DNS, Verify Certificate, View Headers, and Unshorten URL provide focused investigations outside the main scan flow.

                                9

                                Know the data status

                                Local means processed on the device. Live means returned by an external source. Cached means previously retrieved. Partial means some sources succeeded and others did not. Unavailable means a specific source failed while the rest of the report can continue.
