# Proxuma IT v3.54.3 — Step 5 Portrait Readable Scroll

This update repairs the Step 5 mobile portrait layout after testing showed the heading and intro copy could still collapse into stacked letters.

## Changes

- Makes the entire Step 5 workspace horizontally scrollable in portrait mode.
- Gives Step 5 a readable minimum portrait width instead of forcing text into very narrow columns.
- Keeps the heading, intro sentence, nav links, findings filters, and findings cards in normal horizontal writing mode.
- Keeps landscape mode responsive and friendly without forcing the wider portrait rail.
- Updates the CSS cache-buster to `assets/css/styles.css?v=3.54.3`.
